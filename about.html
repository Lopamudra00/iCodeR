What is Your Friendd?

Your Friendd is an online consultation platform for the people who go through Depression and Anxiety. We all know that in the modern era, a lot of people go through these problems and the nail on head is that people scupper to rent out their tales to anyone as they are scared of being called as 'Mentally ill'. It's hard to change the perception of society and that's why we've come up with Your Friend, where people can register themselves for consultation from Medical Consultants and if required, from the world renowned doctors also.The people who don't want to disclose their identity can submit their problem anonynously and they'll get an equal response. It's upto the user to choose pharmacological or non-pharmocological intervention, but we recommend the latter. We hold your hand, support you morally and help you out.

Why Your Friendd?

We lend our shoulders to your problems. We're completely aware that a pretty good number of people go through Depression and Anxiety and maximum among them scupper to tell their parents and friends about it. Such people are welcomed by Your Friendd and are connected to the best mental consultants and doctors who give their best to help them out. We don't disclose the identity of those who don't want to do so. What matter for us is to provide support and we can do it without even knowing you. People can also be social, post their views and connect to people across the globe. Moreover, the depressed people would get a platform to rent out their tales to the world, if required anonymously, and feel relaxed as we know that sharing lessens the grief. Join your hands with ours and join the hearts.
